What is Generative AI Chatbot?

A Generative AI Chatbot is an artificial intelligence-powered conversational agent that uses generative models to generate human-like responses. It is trained on large datasets and learns patterns, language, and context to produce coherent and contextually relevant responses. Generative AI Chatbots have applications in customer support, virtual assistants, and various other domains where human-like interactions are desired.
